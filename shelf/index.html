AXIOMORT™

AXIOMORT™ is a pending trademark application in Ireland (currently in opposition period).

It is about a lion the size of a universe quantum-linked to a man in Ireland, DARB, who is the character based on my own likeness.

The cosmic Lion gets infected by a black ink parasite that consumes the main characters within the lion. The parasite consumes each Main Character Wasteland_Woman, Cosmic Jerry, Zavion, Sylvara, Malik Creed, Nyther Solis and Maya.

After he consumes the crystals he becomes human; his human alias is Evander Questborne. He must go to the lion's brain area to overthrow the mind throne using the power he consumed and become the man that is connected to the lion to gain the power of the lion.

However, throughout the series DARB is scammed 4 times, because the energy dissipates into DARB getting scammed instead of consumed, because he has the lion power.

But because Evander Questborne has the crystal souls, he's able to gain enough power to use the mind gate to manifest himself into DARB's real life. So he pulls off the 4th scam this time setting up a scam syndicate, a scam so big it could overthrow and destroy DARB's influence once and for all. DARB eventually has to conclude that after these scams, and after the Garda (Irish police) won't do anything about it, he must reconnect with his childhood self the one who has the knowledge of his inner ability.

Simultaneously the lion, in the above structure (as above, so below), this entire time was climbing a mountain. At this point in the series, the lion reaches the mountain top and releases the parasite. Evander Questborne's plans go down the gutter as his office space is consumed by a tribal force initiated by the lion, and sacrificed at the altar which means the Celtic curse of financial distress is finally broken. DARB finds himself rummaging through old notebooks of his childhood drawings, bringing old characters back to life in new cinematic forms for the pilot and episode 2.
