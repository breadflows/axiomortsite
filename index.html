Dimensional Brief

What Remains After the Collapse
ENTRY_001

The Collapse

Gravity no longer obeys consensus. Spatial rules shift between rooms. What was once floor becomes void. Pilots report corridors folding into themselves without warning.

ENTRY_002

Data Shards

Fragments of the old world's source code, scattered across the inverted maze. Each shard holds a piece of what reality used to be. Collect enough and the pattern starts to emerge.

ENTRY_003

The Maze

Not a structure — a living system. It reconfigures based on the pilot's movement, as if it learns. Some theorize the maze is what's left of a mind that once held all of this together.

ENTRY_004

Axiomort Protocol

A last-resort procedure encoded before the collapse. Its full contents are still being decrypted. What's been recovered suggests it was designed to invert inversion itself.
